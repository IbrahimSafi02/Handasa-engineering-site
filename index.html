About

Design-minded engineers for real-world constraints.

Handasa Engineering Consultants is a design and development engineering firm focused on transforming ideas into manufacturable, validated, and practical products. Our work combines mechanical design, CAD modeling, engineering analysis, prototyping, and research & development to help clients move efficiently from concept to implementation. Drawing upon experience in product development, simulation, and manufacturing-oriented design, Handasa seeks to provide solutions that balance innovation, practicality, and long-term reliability.

Modern engineering projects face an increasingly difficult environment. Products are expected to reach market faster, meet stricter performance requirements, minimize cost, and maintain reliability throughout their service life. Many promising concepts fail not because of a lack of innovation, but because design assumptions are never properly validated, prototypes are not optimized, or development costs become prohibitive. Handasa exists to bridge this gap by providing structured engineering support throughout the development process, reducing uncertainty and helping clients make informed technical decisions before committing significant resources.

Project Catalogue

Project: Jallab, advanced vertical axis wind turbine.

Compact renewable power through vertical-axis turbine design

Vertical axis wind turbines offer a valuable path for distributed renewable power because they can accept wind from changing directions, operate in constrained locations, and reduce the need for complex yaw systems. Their geometry makes them especially relevant for urban, semi-urban, remote, and infrastructure-adjacent settings where conventional horizontal-axis turbines may be less practical.

Jallab approaches the VAWT problem through a helical blade architecture, protected ring structure, central shaft integration, and simulation-led design validation. Instead of treating the turbine as only an aerodynamic object, our development process studies the full mechanical system: torque transfer, blade strain, tensile loading, fatigue life, manufacturability, and long-term structural reliability.

The product is intended to offer a compact, durable, and scalable turbine platform for clients seeking renewable energy systems that are easier to site, easier to prototype, and suitable for continued engineering refinement. Jallab combines CAD development, structural simulation, lifecycle review, and practical manufacturing planning into a turbine concept designed for real-world testing and eventual deployment.

  • Helical blade geometry and shaft integration
  • Stress, deflection, torque, and fatigue-oriented review
  • Prototype-focused CAD and manufacturability planning
  • Renewable energy system development for practical deployment
